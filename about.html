About Us - VibeBot-AI Sentiment Analyzer

Project Overview

VibeBot-AI is a sentiment analysis tool designed to help users explore customer sentiments and product recommendations based on reviews. By leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, we aim to provide insights that enhance the shopping experience.

Problem Statement

In today's market, consumers often struggle to make informed purchasing decisions due to overwhelming choices and varying product reviews. Our goal is to simplify this process by providing data-driven insights that help users understand product sentiments and make better choices.
